IV. ab压测工具

ab压测工具

ab压测出现"Failed Requests"的情况：

Failed requests:        101
(Connect: 0, Receive: 0, Length: 101, Exceptions: 0)
  • Connect: 连接失败
  • Receive: 没有收到返回
  • Length: 返回长度和之前不一致
  • Exceptions: 未预期错误
